Chicken Curry recipe


Chicken curry

Rich, bold, and bursting with flavor — this chicken curry is the ultimate comfort dish. Tender chicken pieces simmered in a silky, spiced tomato-coconut gravy that clings to every bite with warmth and depth. Aromatic garlic, ginger, and garam masala dance together in perfect harmony, making each mouthful a savory explosion. Serve it with fluffy rice or buttery naan and watch it disappear — this one’s pure soul food.
