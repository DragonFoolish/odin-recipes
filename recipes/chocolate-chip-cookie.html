Chocolate Chip Cookies

Thick, golden, and irresistibly gooey — these chocolate chip cookies are made for true cookie lovers and big bakers who don’t settle for average. Every bite is packed with melty chocolate, soft centers, and buttery edges that hit just right. Whether you’re baking for the fam or flexing your oven skills, this recipe brings that homemade magic that tastes like comfort and nostalgia. Big batch or small, these cookies disappear fast.


Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ cups (280g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup (226g) unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup (165g) packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups (340g) semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Crushed carmel bits
